A Little About Me

My name is Alea and I am a product designer located in Seattle, WA. I have over 3 years of professional design experience, specializing in mobile app and web design. I recieved a BA from Kenyon College, graduating with degrees in art and psychology. I also recieved a UX Design and Development Certificate from Code Fellows. My cumulative work and educational experiences allow me to have a deeper understanding of why people find certain things aesthetically pleasing, and I use this to create effective products.

In my free time I like to do things outdoors like hiking and kayaking, read, cook and eat, craft, do art, scuba dive, and travel.






Work process

This is a simplified process, but I like to be involved in multiple aspects of product design. I am a well rounded designer who likes to follow the progress of my product from an idea to shipment.

1. Discuss

I always try to talk things out before I start designing. Whether that is with PMs, shareholders, or users, I think fully understanding the product and its perameters is incredibly important in creative effective, user-friendly designs.

2. Prototype

I like to create designs that resemble the final product as closely as possible. I aim to make my designs interactive; this can range from designing web items in code to using InVision or Axure to make still images interactive. If working with a research team, I will then pass these off to them to conduct a series of studies on the designs.

3. Reiterate

After the initial designs have been finished, I collect feedback from peers and user research results. This invaluable feedback helps me to look back at my designs from a more educated perspective and I often find myself redesigning many aspects of the products with this new information.
